Renjie Wan is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science of Hong Kong Baptist University.

He obtained his Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University in 2019.

Before joining HKBU, he was a Wallenberg-NTU Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow of Nanyang Technological University from 2020 to 2022.
