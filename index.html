I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science of Hong Kong Baptist University.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from NTU in 2019 supervised by Prof. Alex C. Kot.

Before joining HKBU, I was a Wallenberg-NTU Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow of Nanyang Technological University from 2020 to 2022.

I have several Ph.D. openings at this moment. If you want to work with me in the future, please feel free to drop me an email. All emails/CVs are carefully read and evaluated. Only matched candidates will be responded.
